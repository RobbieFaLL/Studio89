Nail Technician

A nail technician, or nail tech, is a professional who specializes in the care and beautification of clients' nails. They perform manicures, pedicures, nail art, and other nail treatments, ensuring that nails are healthy and aesthetically pleasing.

Nail techs are skilled in various techniques, including nail shaping, cuticle care, and applying nail polish, gel, or acrylics. They also provide advice on nail care and maintenance.
