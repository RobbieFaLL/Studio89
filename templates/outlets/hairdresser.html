Hairdresser

A hairdresser is a professional who specializes in cutting, coloring, and styling hair to enhance a client's appearance. They are skilled in various techniques and trends,

providing personalized services to meet each client's needs and preferences. Hairdressers often work in salons, where they also offer advice on hair care and maintenance.
