Tatooist

A tattooist, also known as a tattoo artist, is a skilled professional who creates permanent designs on the skin using needles and ink. They work closely with clients to develop custom artwork that reflects personal stories, interests, or aesthetics.

Tattooists must have a strong artistic ability, attention to detail, and a thorough understanding of hygiene and safety practices to ensure a safe and satisfying experience for their clients.
